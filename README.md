# Privacy Policy for ReceiptRebate

**Effective Date: April 23, 2026**

ReceiptRebate is built with a privacy-first philosophy. We believe your shopping habits are your business.

### 1. Data Collection
We do not collect, store, or transmit any Personal Identifiable Information (PII). 
* **No Accounts:** You do not need to provide a name, phone number, or address to use the app.
* **Email:** Your email is only requested at the moment of cash-out and is used solely by our payout partner (Tremendous) to deliver your reward. We do not store this email on our servers.

### 2. On-Device Processing
All receipt scanning and text recognition (OCR) are performed **locally on your device**. No images of your receipts are ever uploaded, stored, or shared.

### 3. Data Usage
To facilitate the rebate economy, the app transmits anonymized, non-identifiable purchase data (e.g., "Grocery match in Zip Code 93301") to our partners. This data cannot be linked back to you.

### 4. Data Deletion
All transaction history is stored locally on your device. To delete all data, use the "Clear All Data" button in the App Settings or uninstall the application.

### 5. Contact
For questions regarding this policy, contact us at codebluetodev@gmail.com.
